Exercise Tips

And Guides

Want to go out to the park and exercise, but don't know how to start? Well, don't worry because we have got some guides and tips for you!
So just take the first step towards a healthier lifestyle.

Tips & Guides

Don't try too hard

Take things step by step. You don't need to make big actions to make significant progress, all you need is consistency and effort and overtime, you will be getting the results you want. This not a race.

Start with what you have right now

You don't need fancy stuff like digital tools made for exercising. Start with what you have now and make as much progress as you can.

Work outs you can do in parks

Bench Dips

Benches are a versatile amenities in the park, not only can you sit on the benches, you can also use it to your advantage to train your muscles. The exercise itself is also fairly simple, you can even do it at home if you want.

Bench Push ups

There are also exercises that you can do with benches. People are already familiary with the exercise shown here, it is simple yet effective. By using the bench to do push ups, it becomes easier so that even beginners can manage it.
